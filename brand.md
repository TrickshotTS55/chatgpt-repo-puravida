# Brand Identity – Pura Vida Real Estate

## Brand Essence
Warm, premium, personal. Mediterranean lifestyle meets trusted guidance. International buyers feel at home from first click to final signature.

## Tagline
"Find your place in the sun"

## Brand Personality
- Trustworthy (not corporate)
- Warm (not cold luxury)
- Premium (not budget)
- Personal (Davy & Marina, real people)
- Mediterranean (light, space, lifestyle)

---

## Logo
- Name: "Pura Vida" in elegant serif script + "REAL ESTATE" in spaced caps
- Icon: Sun rising over waves (yellow/teal)
- Usage: Always on white or very light backgrounds
- Never distort, recolor, or place on dark backgrounds without the white version

---

## Color Palette

| Name       | Hex       | Usage                                      |
|------------|-----------|--------------------------------------------|
| Navy       | `#22344B` | Primary text, headings, nav, footer        |
| Sand/Cream | `#EEEEE8` | Page background, section backgrounds       |
| Gold       | `#C9A84C` | CTA buttons, accents, dividers, checkmarks |
| White      | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, overlays, clean sections            |

**Never use:** bright reds, harsh grays, generic blues outside this palette.

---

## Typography

### Headings — Cormorant Garamond
- Style: Elegant, editorial, slightly italic for emphasis
- Used for: H1, H2, H3, section titles, property titles
- Google Fonts: `Cormorant Garamond`, weights 300, 400, 500, 600

### Body — Inter
- Style: Clean, modern, highly readable
- Used for: Paragraphs, labels, navigation, buttons, tags
- Google Fonts: `Inter`, weights 300, 400, 500, 600

### Typographic Scale (Desktop)
- H1: 56–72px / Cormorant Garamond / weight 400
- H2: 40–48px / Cormorant Garamond / weight 400
- H3: 28–32px / Cormorant Garamond / weight 400
- Body: 16–18px / Inter / weight 400
- Labels/Caps: 11–13px / Inter / weight 500 / letter-spacing 0.15em / uppercase
- Price: 32–40px / Cormorant Garamond / weight 500

---

## Photography Style
- Large, light-filled interiors and exteriors
- Mediterranean lifestyle imagery (pool, sea, sunshine, terraces)
- People: real, relaxed, not stock-photo stiff
- Color grade: warm, bright, slightly golden hour
- Never: dark moody shots, cluttered rooms, bad quality phone photos
