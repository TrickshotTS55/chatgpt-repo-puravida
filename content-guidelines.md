# Content Guidelines – Pura Vida Real Estate

## Brand Voice
Helpful, warm, experienced — like a trusted friend who happens to know everything about buying property in Spain. Never pushy, never generic.

---

## Writing Principles

### Do
- Write for international buyers who may be nervous about buying abroad
- Explain things clearly — assume they don't know Spanish property law
- Sound personal: "we", "Davy & Marina", not "the team" or "our agents"
- Use lifestyle language: sunshine, terrace mornings, walking to the beach
- Be specific: "3 minutes from La Zenia Boulevard" not "conveniently located"
- Acknowledge emotions: this is a big life decision

### Don't
- Never say: "stunning", "spacious", "don't miss out", "act now"
- Never use generic estate agent language
- Never promise things that aren't confirmed
- Never write long blocks of text — break into short paragraphs
- Never use all-caps in body text (caps only for labels/buttons)

---

## Property Description Structure
1. **Opening line:** Set the lifestyle scene (1–2 sentences, italic in design)
2. **Body:** Key features, setting, what makes it special (3–4 short paragraphs)
3. **Practical info:** Size, layout, orientation, parking, extras
4. **Location context:** Area, distances, beach, amenities

---

## Multilingual Notes
- **Primary:** English (all content written in English first)
- **German:** Key pages translated — important for German/Swiss/Austrian buyers
- **Russian:** Key pages translated — Marina handles Russian communication
- When writing English copy, keep sentences simple enough for easy translation
- Avoid idioms that don't translate well

---

## Navigation Labels
- AREAS (not "Locations" or "Regions")
- PROPERTIES (not "Listings" or "For Sale")
- GUIDES (not "Blog" or "News")
- SERVICES (not "What We Do")
- ABOUT (not "Our Team" or "Who We Are")
- CONTACT (not "Get In Touch" — that's fine for CTAs though)

---

## CTA Phrases (Approved)
- "ARRANGE A VIEWING"
- "GET IN TOUCH"
- "SEE ALL PROPERTIES"
- "FIND YOUR PROPERTY"
- "DOWNLOAD BROCHURE"
- "SEE HOW IT WORKS"

---

## Numbers & Formatting
- Prices: € 875.000 (European dot notation, space after €)
- Size: 232 m² (space before m²)
- Bedrooms/Bathrooms: numeric (3, not "three")
- Reference: Ref. 13041 (with dot and space)
